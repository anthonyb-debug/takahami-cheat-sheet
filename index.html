Process & Work Flows
// Follow these steps for every claim review and action
1

Access the Spreadsheet or Account

You will be advised to access a spreadsheet or an account will be given to you directly by the Onshore Manager.

2

Review ALL Claims

ALL CLAIMS need to be reviewed — Status, Claim Notes, Appeal Documents, Signed Contracts, Web Portals.

3

Check Claim Statuses

If status of all claims are correct, jump to Step 5 to choose which process you're about to do. Otherwise, proceed with the next step.

4

Request Onshore Approval

Request approval from the onshore manager which claims you will be changing and for what reason. Once approved, proceed to Step 5. If not, endorse the claims to the Onshore Manager.

5

Choose Your Process

Your process will depend on whether negotiation is allowed, especially if there is a Pricing Vendor (TPA).

5.A — NEGOTIATIONS

Identify which Pricing Vendor to contact and how to submit the negotiation inquiry. See the Pricing Vendors tab for contact info.

5.B — APPEALS

View the list of carriers and workflow in creating the appeal. See the Carriers tab for contact info including Appeal TFL and TAT.

Negotiation Thresholds
// CPT/HCPCS threshold amounts by code type
CA Claims: All CA claims have the same threshold of at least 50% of the billed amount and a target of 65% of the billed amount.
UMR Claims (Detox & Residential): At least $5,000 or 65% of the billed amount even if Non-CA — whichever is higher.
MA Claims: Follow Non-CA rules except for Detoxification and Residential which should be at least 50% of the billed amount and target of 65% — whichever is higher.
